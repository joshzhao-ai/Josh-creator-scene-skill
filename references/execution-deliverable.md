## Calibration order

1. Turn off decorative lights; set camera exposure and white balance.
2. Place and diffuse the key; make skin exposure and catchlight correct.
3. Add negative fill or weak fill to shape the shadow side.
4. Add the practical/background light; lower it until it supports instead of competing.
5. Add only enough rim for shoulder/hair separation.
6. Remove one background object if the eye does not return to the face.

## Final test frame

Ask for a 10-second test clip or a representative video frame after setup. Diagnose in this order: face exposure, key direction, white balance, background brightness, shelf/plant spacing, then crop. Do not buy more gear before this test.
